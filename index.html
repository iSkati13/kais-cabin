How to Get Here

Private Vehicle (From Manila or North Luzon)
  1. Exit NLEX at Bocaue.
  2. From Manila: Turn right onto Gov. Fortunato Halili Ave.
    From North Luzon: Turn left onto Gov. Fortunato Halili Ave.
  3. Continue straight, pass BMMG Hospital, Mateo Hospital.
  4. Turn left onto Santa Maria Bypass Road (Landmarks: PTT, Shakey's, Contis, Peri Peri).
  5. Merge onto Norzagaray - Santa Maria Road (Landmark: Waltermart Sta. Maria).
  6. Go straight, pass Goldilocks, Philhealth, RePhil Gas station.
  7. Turn onto City Land Drive (Landmarks: AllHome, Jollibee, McDo).
  8. Pin Pulong Buhangin Barangay Hall for Waze/Google Maps.
  9. Straight along Cityland Drive, turn left on Lopez Jaena before Divine Mercy chapel to reach Kai's Cabin.
Commute
  1. Ride a bus at Trinoma P2P terminal bound to Sta. Maria (Precious Grace Transport).
  2. Drop at Sta. Clara or Caypombo, then ride a Jeep/Bus to Norzagaray.
  3. Ask to be dropped at City Land or Pulong Buhangin Barangay Hall.
  4. Ride a trike to Kai's Cabin.
